The Little Prince

"Standing with quiet confidence,
you remind me of the Little Prince’s belief
that true style isn’t about appearances,
but about the story behind them.
Every detail — the bold stripes,
the silver chain, the thoughtful pose —
feels like a reflection of individuality
that can’t be seen with just the eyes.
As the Prince said,
“what is essential is invisible,”
and here, the essential is your presence:
a mix of strength, curiosity, and authenticity
that shines through beyond the outfit itself."
